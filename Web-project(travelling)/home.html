Myanmar culture

Our culture embraces visitors with warmth and hospitality.

Myanmar's culture is a colorful mix of different traditions and influences.
During festivals , there are lively parades and dances in the streets ,
and the sound of temple bells adds to the peaceful atmosphere.
Traditional arts, such as making lacquerware and puppets, are still
very much a part of daily life, and the people here are known for their
friendly and welcoming nature. Myanmar combines its ancient heritage
with modern elements to create a fascinating cultural experience that everyone can enjoy.
